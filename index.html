What Should We Do Next

Strategic recommendations based on comprehensive data analysis to drive growth, improve customer satisfaction, and optimize marketing efforts

Product Strategy

  • Expand premium outerwear line targeting 35-44 age demographic
  • Develop sustainable product lines to capitalize on 38% growth trend
  • Improve product descriptions to reduce 3-star reviews by 27%
  • Create bundled offerings based on shopping basket analysis

Marketing Strategy

  • Targeted promotions (15-20% off) for high-value customer segments
  • Regional marketing campaigns tailored to coastal vs. midwestern preferences
  • Seasonal marketing aligning with category demand peaks
  • Focus on mobile shopping experience (68% of users prefer mobile)

Customer Experience

  • Optimize delivery process to address 32% of negative reviews
  • Improve customer service response times for higher satisfaction scores
  • Enhance omnichannel experience (online + offline integration)
  • Implement loyalty programs for 25-44 age groups (highest spending)
